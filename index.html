Case Study 6

The Real Estate Repositioning Play

Project: Royal Greens by Man Developers

Client: Indoprops

The Challenge

Lead generation was failing. The market perceived the project's location (Dewas Naka) as too far from Indore's main hub (Vijaynagar), creating a significant barrier to purchase. The core problem wasn't the product; it was the perception.

My Role

Lead Strategist & Campaign Architect

My Solution

I pivoted the entire strategy. Instead of trying to sell the township (the product), I developed a campaign to sell the area (the solution).

  • Reframe the Narrative: Promoting the area first as the new extension of Vijaynagar.
  • Sell the Why: Sold the concept—peace of mind, community living, and an escape from the city's congestion.
  • Introduce the How: Only after establishing the area's value did we introduce Royal Greens.

The Result

The narrative shift was a success. By identifying and solving the core location objection before the sales pitch, we broke the lead-gen stalemate.
